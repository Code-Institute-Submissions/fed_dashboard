Fig.7 illustrates that while more male pupils achieve the basic grades, significantly more female pupils achieve the higher grades.

Criterion 1: Candidates who received a minimum of 5 grade D3s (40 - 49.9%) at any level

Criterion 2: Candidates who received a minimum of 6 grade D3s (40 - 49.9%) at any level of which 2 or more were grade C3 (55 - 59.9) or above on higher papers

Criterion 3: Candidates who received a minimum of 6 grade D3s (40 - 49.9%) at any level of which 4 or more were grade C3 (55 - 59.9) or above on higher papers

Criterion 4: Candidates who received a minimum of 6 grade C3s (55 - 59.9) on higher papers

Criterion 5: Candidates who received a minimum of 6 grade C3s (55 - 59.9) on higher papers of which 3 or more were grade B3s (70 - 74.9)or above

Criterion 6: Candidates who received a minimum of 6 grade C3s (55 - 59.9) on higher papers of which 3 or more were grade A2s (85 - 89.9)or above
